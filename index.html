Knowledge Graphs Vocabulary

This vocabulary was created as part of the Knowledge Graphs course. It defines classes and properties for describing persons and organizations.

Namespace

https://jeroen-demeester.github.io/knowledge-graphs-vocab/vocab#

Classes

  • Person – A human individual
  • Organization – A company or institution
  • Museum – A type of organization

Properties

  • name – Name of a person or organization
  • birthDate – Birth date of a person
  • worksAt – Employment relation
  • knows – Relation between persons
